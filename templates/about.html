About the Project

AI for healthier crops, stronger harvests

TomatoAI is a deep learning research tool built to help farmers and agronomists quickly identify tomato leaf diseases through computer vision.

Our mission

Tomato crops are one of the most important food sources globally, yet diseases can wipe out up to 80% of a harvest if left untreated. Early detection is everything.

TomatoAI puts a research-grade AI model in the hands of anyone with a smartphone — no lab equipment, no agronomist on speed-dial. Just a photo of a leaf and a result in seconds.

This project is built for educational and research use, combining modern deep learning techniques with an accessible, mobile-friendly interface.

🌱

"Early detection saves harvests — and livelihoods."

How it works

Three simple steps from photo to diagnosis.

1

Upload a leaf image

Take a clear photo of the tomato leaf in good lighting. Drag and drop it onto the upload zone or tap to browse from your camera roll.

2

AI analysis

The image is passed through a convolutional neural network trained on the PlantVillage dataset. The model extracts visual features and computes probabilities across all disease classes.

3

Get your result

The top predicted class is returned instantly — whether that's "Healthy" or a specific disease name. Use this as a starting point for further investigation or treatment planning.
